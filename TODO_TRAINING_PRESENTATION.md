### Engagement Techniques
- Use real-world examples from the organization
- Encourage questions throughout the session

- Test all demo scenarios beforehand

- Build in breaks for longer sessions
- Be flexible with pacing based on audience

- Ensure screen readability
- Provide large print handouts
- Consider different learning styles
- Offer follow-up sessions for different time zones
